Expo Overview


China-ASEAN Mining Cooperation Forum & Exhibition is a national level international regional mining economic and trade exchange event sponsored by Ministry of Natural Resources of China and People’s Government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and organized by Guangxi Department of Land and Resources, China Geological Survey, Department of Science, Technology & International Cooperation Ministry of Natural Resources, the Land and Resources Department of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, China Geological Survey, Science and Technology Cooperation Department of the Ministry of Natural Resources, Guangxi Bureau of Geology & Mineral Prospecting & Exploitation, and Guangxi Geological Mining Investment Group Co., Ltd. Since 2010, it has been held annually in Nanning, Guangxi.
       China-ASEAN Mining Forum is one of the important events under the framework of China-ASEAN Mining Cooperation Forum & Exhibition and is the official forum hosted by the Ministry of Natural Resources of the P.R. China. China-ASEAN Mining New Technology, Mining Machinery & Gems Expo (China-ASEAN Mining Expo for short) is held at the same time every year focusing on mining forum. Through the organic combination of the Mining Forum and the Mining Exhibition, they promote each other and build a new platform for international exchange, cooperation and communication between officials, enterprises and experts and scholars of the Chinese and ASEAN countries in mining industry, and help China and ASEAN and the countries along the Belt and Road with the economic and trade cooperation and development.
       Name in Chinese：中国-东盟矿业合作论坛暨推介展示会
       Name in English：China-ASEAN Mining Cooperation Forum & Exhibition
       Short in Chinese：China-ASEAN Mining Forum
       Short in English：CAMCFE
       Venue：Nanning International Convention and Exhibition Center
       Achievements in past years: Since 2010, China-ASEAN Mining Forum has been successfully held in Nanning for 8 consecutive years. Through the holding of previous forums, many cooperation results have been achieved. Over the past 8 years, the Forum has received more than 7,100 delegates, more than 2,720 participating companies, more than 1,270 projects for promotion and signing projects of118. The total contracted amount is about 55.30 billion yuan (amounting to 8.70 billion US dollars).
       Positioning: With the purpose of promoting the construction of China-ASEAN Free Trade Area and sharing opportunities for cooperation and development, focusing on the principle of mutual benefit stipulated by Framework Agreement on Comprehensive Economic Co-operation between the People’s Republic of China and the Association of South East Asia Nations, with the economic and trade cooperation within the free trade area as the emphasis, open towards the world, and providing new opportunities for the common development of mining companies in various countries.
