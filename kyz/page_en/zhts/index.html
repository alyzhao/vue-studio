Contents: High-level forum, merchandise trade, investment cooperation, services trade, cultural exchanges, exhibition and sales services

Characteristics:

1. Combining import and export, featuring imports, emphasizing opening up to the ASEAN market and serving as a bridge for ASEAN mining commodities to enter China.

2. Combining investment and investment promotion, featuring “going global” of Chinese enterprises, and serving as a platform for Chinese enterprises in the mining industry to invest in ASEAN.

3. Combining merchandise trade and service trade, with mining service and transfer of technological innovations of SMEs as an entry point, fostering new growth points for China-ASEAN mining economic and trade cooperation.

4. The combination of exhibitions and conferences complement each other. 2018 (the 9th) China-ASEAN Mining Cooperation Forum & Exhibition and 2018 China-ASEAN Mining New Technology, Mining Machinery & Gems Expo are to be held concurrently, and the two are organically combined and promoted. There are both real economic and trade activities, as well as mutual dialogue and exchanges between officials, enterprises, experts and scholars.

5. Mining events and diplomatic arena. The Mining Forum is both a mining event and a multilateral international event, which enhances understanding and fully reflects the purpose and intention of China-ASEAN good-neighborly friendship and the establishment of a strategic partnership of peace and prosperity, and has pragmatically promoted the in-depth development of regional economic mining cooperation between China and ASEAN countries.

6. The combination of economic and trade activities and cultural exchanges. During the Forum, “2018 China-ASEAN International Jewelry Creative Design and Production Contest”, “2018 China-ASEAN Man and Nature Photography Contest”, and brand jewelry model show, etc., and other colorful cultural activities will be held at the same time.

7. While doing on-site exhibition and marketing services, we will focus on providing sales and service before and after the exhibition. The forum is the platform, transaction is the link, and win-win is the consensus.
