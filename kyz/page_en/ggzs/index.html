III. Sponsorship Items

    The Exhibition Organizing Committee is open for sponsorship and will give the naming rights, advertisement release and other returns on the basis of the application and review, including:

    1. Two Strategic Partners for 2018 CAMCF, with 13 perks including a free speech at specified sub-forum, video advertisement before the expo, a 72-square-meter ready-made booth, the right to conduct business promotion activities using the brand of “Strategic Partner of the Mining Forum” in the year of 2018, a naming business party, and rolling advertising on the online forum live broadcast platform, altogether 13 items in return.

    2. Ten Supporting Companies for 2018 CAMCF, with 10 perks including a free speech at specified sub-forum, video advertisement after the expo, a 36-square-meter ready-made stand and the right to conduct business promotion activities using the brand of “Strategic Partner of the Mining Forum” in the year of 2018, and rolling advertising on the online forum live broadcast platform, altogether 10 items in return.

    3. Several Sponsor Companies for 2018 CAMCF, with 4 to 7 perks of different kinds including choice to provide tea break, bottle waters, bags, file containers, pens, uniforms for volunteers and other materials, the title of “Sponsor unit” for 2018 CAMCF.
