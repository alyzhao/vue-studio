I. Preference for early payment

Individual exhibitors: Enterprises that have paid the full amount before August 30, 2018 will be offered a discount of 600 yuan for each standard booth, and a discount of 80 yuan/m2 for the raw space;

Group exhibitors: Enterprises that have paid the full amount before August 30, 2018 will be offered a discount of 300 yuan for each standard booth, and a discount of 30 yuan/m2 for the raw space;

Concentration exhibitors: Enterprises that have paid the full amount before August 30, 2018 will be offered a discount of 300 yuan for each enterprise.

II. Preference for famous brands

(1) High-tech enterprises such as energy-saving technologies and equipment, energy-efficient products, energy-saving service industries, advanced environmental protection technologies and equipment, environmental protection products and environmental protection services, and resource recycling and other key technologies and equipment will be given a 30% discount;

(2) Enterprises enjoying preference for the national well-known trademarks (within the valid period) identified by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce will be given a 20% discount;

(3) Enterprises with provincial well-known trademarks, provincial well-known products and provincial well-known firm will be given a 10% discount;

(4) Enterprises with municipal or prefecture level well-known trademarks or products will be given a 5% discount.

Note: The above-mentioned four preferences exclude fees for double opening.

III. Preference for leading enterprises

(1) The World’s Top 500 Companies enjoy free booths (limited to 36m2);

(2) Industry associations of national first-level or vice president and over enjoy a 20% discount;

(3) Industry associations of provincial first-level or vice president and over enjoy a 10% discount;

Note: The above-mentioned three preferences exclude fees for double opening.

IV. Instructions for handling the above mentioned preferential policies

(1) Eligible enterprises may enjoy the above preferential policies and go through the formalities according to the following order: Item 1, preference for early payment → Item 2, preference for brands, Item 3, preference for leading enterprises;

(2) Enterprises cannot enjoy preferential policies for brand and for leading enterprises at the same time, and high preferential policies are implemented;

(3) Enterprises that are eligible to enjoy preferential policies for brand and leading enterprises shall file the application upon the valid relevant certificate, the original of the certificate or the copies of the relevant materials stamped with the official seal of the company when they pay booth fees and will not enjoy the preferential policies overdue.

V. Exhibition condition

    Exhibitors must have legal business qualifications in the country (region) of registration, provide a scanned copy of the original business license, and indicate “We declare that this document is consistent with the original, true and effective” and then stamp the official seal.
